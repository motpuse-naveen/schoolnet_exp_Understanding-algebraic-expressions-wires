Understanding Algebraic Expressions (Wires)

In this exploriment you have to arrange the coloured wires to match the given algebraic expression.

a = length of Red wire
b = length of Green wire
c = length of Blue wire
d = length of Purple wire
unit length of wire
1 cm
Score

Instructions

Each coloured wire is represented by a letter, for e.g. letter 'a' represents the red wire. You are given an algebraic expression, as a question, which is displayed in the text box below.

Drag and drop the correct wires into the grey-coloured equation chamber and press OK.
